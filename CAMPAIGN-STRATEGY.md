# QuoteFollow — Campaign Strategy

## The Pitch

**One-liner:** "We follow up on every quote you send so you never lose a job to silence."

**Value prop:** Contractors send quotes but follow up 0-1 times. We do 4 follow-ups over 18 days. Result: higher close rates, more revenue, zero effort.

**Price:**
- Starter: $149/mo (up to 30 quotes tracked)
- Growth: $249/mo (up to 80 quotes, priority follow-ups)
- Setup fee: $99 (one-time)

## Cold Email Strategy

### Target List Building
1. Google Maps scraping for HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, landscaping
2. Cities: Austin, Dallas, Houston, Phoenix, Denver, Nashville, Charlotte, Atlanta
3. Filter: businesses with websites (need email addresses)
4. Goal: 500 leads in week 1

### Email Sequences

**Sequence 1: Loss Aversion (Primary)**
- Email 1: "You sent 47 quotes last month — how many did you follow up on twice?"
- Focus: money left on the table, specific dollar amounts
- Expected reply rate: 3-5%

**Sequence 2: Case Study**
- Email 1: "How a roofing company increased close rate by 89%"
- Focus: social proof, specific results
- Expected reply rate: 4-6%

**Sequence 3: Statistics**
- Email 1: "{business} is losing $3,200/month from cold quotes"
- Focus: data-driven, personalized
- Expected reply rate: 2-4%

### Follow-up Sequence (for replies)
1. Same day: Quick response, book a call
2. Day 1: Send a sample follow-up sequence they'd get
3. Day 3: Case study + testimonial
4. Day 7: Final offer with discount

## Conversion Funnel

```
Cold Email (80/week)
  → Reply Rate: 4% = 3.2 replies/week
    → Call Booked: 50% = 1.6 calls/week
      → Close Rate: 30% = 0.5 clients/week
        → Monthly: ~2 new clients/month
```
